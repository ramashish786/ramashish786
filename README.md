## About Me 
AI / Machine Learning Engineer with 4.6 years of experience building and deploying production-grade ML and GenAI systems across finance, marketing, and analytics domains. Proven expertise in designing end-to-end pipelines that move from raw data to structured insight to autonomous action — including Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines, multi-agent AI workflows (LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI), semantic search with vector databases, and prompt engineering/fine-tuning of transformer-based models (BERT, GPT, LLaMA). Skilled in PyTorch, TensorFlow, dimensional data modeling, and scalable ML deployment on AWS with MPP / distributed data warehouses. Experienced in collaborating with platform, data engineering, and business teams to translate ambiguous problems into reliable, grounded, and observable AI solutions with measurable business impact.
